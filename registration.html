Registration

Authors should read the policy for accepted papers.

If you require a support letter for your Australian visitor's visa, please check the appropriate box during registration.

Please email DSN2024Brisbane@gmail.com if you need any registration assistance. For questions regarding author submissions, please contact Hyoungshick Kim (hyoung@skku.edu) and Xingliang Yuan (xingliang.yuan@monash.edu) (our publication chairs).

You can register for DSN 2024 here (Please read the following information before making your booking.): Registration portal

  • Each accepted paper in any track must have a full conference author registration. Each accepted workshop paper must have either a workshop or a full conference author registration.
  • Please double-check the registration type and fee when placing your booking to ensure it matches the corresponding section listed in the table below.
  • If you would like to purchase extra tickets for the banquet, please email dsn2024brisbane@gmail.com with the subject "DSN2024 extra banquet tickets". Ticket price is $130 per person, no children price, GST included.
  • Workshop registration gives access to workshop only (i.e. only on the 24th of June).
  • The early-bird rate ends at midnight on 10 June 2024 midnight AOE. After this date, regular registration fees will apply.

Cancellation policy:

All refund/cancellation requests must be received in writing to UQ Cyber Security DSN2024Brisbane@gmail.com by 24 May, 2024, 11:59 PM AEST Time. There will be an administrative fee of 50% for cancelled registrations. You may only cancel your registration before the cancellation deadline.

Policy for Accepted Papers

  1. Every paper has to have a full registration to appear in the conference proceedings.
  2. The conference is in person and papers are presented only in person.
  3. If none of the authors can come to the conference specifically because none of them could get an Australian visa to attend the conference, then the talk can be delegated to another person that is familiar with the work and can present it in person at the conference. Otherwise, the work will not be presented in the conference but will appear in the proceedings.
