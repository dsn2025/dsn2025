Travel Info

The 54nd Annual IEEE/IFIP International Conference on Dependable Systems and Networks will take place in Pullman Hotel.

Pullman Hotel is located in the heart of Brisbane's CBD. It takes approximately 30 minutes to drive from Brisbane Airport to the hotel. The cost of an Uber ride is around $35, depending on traffic conditions. Alternatively, you can take the Airtrain from the airport to Central Station, which also takes about 30 minutes, with tickets priced at around $20. The hotel is located just a 5-minute walk from Central Station.
